A Quick Introduction to Science!

Science is defined as
the intellectual and practical activity encompassing the systematic study of the structure and behaviour of the physical and natural world through observation and experiment
Because science itself is too broad, people separated the concept into more smaller ones such as physics and chemistry.
By studying more into science, we can know more about the world we live in.
